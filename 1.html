What is HTML?

HyperText Markup Language (HTML) is the most fundamental component of the web, and it's the meaning and structure of web content. Use to define. Other techniques other than HTML are typically used to describe the look/expression (CSS) or function/action (JavaScript) of a web page.

"Hypertext" refers to a link that links a web page to another page. Links are the fundamental attributes of the Web. By posting data on the Internet and linking it to someone else's created pages, you can also become an active member of the World Wide Web world. I have it.
